My Working Process

I follow a structured process to ensure smooth and successful project delivery.

Planning

I begin every project by discussing the client's goals and needs, and creating a detailed plan to achieve them.

Development

Using the latest technologies, I bring the project to life through coding and development.

Testing & Delivery

Before launch, I thoroughly test the project to ensure it meets all specifications and functions properly. Once finalized, I deliver the completed project to the client.

HOW I CODE

I learned web development from the ground up, meaning I am well versed in developing website designs and interfaces from scratch. I actually prefer doing it this way because it gives me more freedom to do something unique than a restrictive template can offer. I always follow a mockup, to ensure I have a clean roadmap for my build. I have redone several outdated websites in the past, and I find it to be a fun time bringing life back into them again.
